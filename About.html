About Us

Our Story

As a child growing up in Nigeria I didn’t have resources to learn about computers and how they operate. The first time I learned about computers and how to use them in college. By that time most of the students in my class were already have knowledge of computers. I decided that I will help any student who is wanting to learn about computers but doesn’t have resources to pay for expensive classes by providing free courses, to give those kids a chance to learn.

I created this website to give people an opportunity to learn basic computer skills, who otherwise would not have a chance. We offer completely FREE class for all those who want to learn. There are 3 courses that you should complete to finish the this program.

If you have a desire to continue and want to learn more advanced skills, please, visit out "Registration" page where you can see what we have available and request more information.

After taking these courses you will be able to have basic understanding of computer hardware, software and system applications which will give you a greater chance in accomplishing what you want. Hopefully you will love STEM and want to pursue it further.
